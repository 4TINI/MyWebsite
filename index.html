My Activities

EMB Student Branch Chapter

Picture from the presentation of the IEEE student Branch Chapter in Politecnico di Milano

BEA (Biomedical Engineering Association)

I'm founder and board member of the association. Our aim is to involve the biomedical engineering students of the University in extra curricular activities.

Basket & Disability

I collaborated on the project "Basket & Disability" promoted by the sport association SMIT ROMA. The initiative was addressed to boys and girls with behavioural and intellectual disability. During the years we participated in various regional and national competitions promoted by the Association "Special Olympics".

Scouting

I started my scouting experience when I was 8 years old. This long lasting experience tought me how to work to achieve goals both in group and on my own in critical situations. Being flexible, open minded and perseverant are all qualities that I learned in my scout group and that I tried to hand down to the kids when I was a chief.

Interniship at IRCCS San Raffaele Pisana

Within the project "Mirror Stroke" I collaborated to design and develop an experimental protocol to implement a neurorehabilitative therapy targeted for post-stroke paralized patients.

Mountaineering

I've always been spellbound by the majesty of mountains. My father has always been very passionated and ever since I was a child he used to bring me with him. He tought me how to climb, to ski, how to tie knots and above all to be humble because the mountain does not forgive those who do not respect it.
